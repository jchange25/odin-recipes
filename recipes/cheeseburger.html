Cheeseburger

Description

A quick and simple recipe that is sure to hit the spot every time.

Its meat, cheese, and bread. What more could you want?

Ingredients:

  • ground beef
  • sliced american cheese
  • hamburger buns
  • seasonings:
    • seasoning salt
    • onion powder
    • garlic powder
    • pepper
  • condiments:
    • ketchup
    • mayonaise
    • mustard

Steps:

  1. Season your ground beef.
  2. Roll the beef into a ball. (The larger the ball, the larger the patty.
  3. Flatten the balls into patties and put a small hole through the center.
  4. Place patties on hot pan and cook on each side for 5 minutes.
  5. When there is one minute left on second side, place cheese on top to melt.
  6. Place burgers on buns, decorate with condiments of your choice, and serve!
