Tuna Sandwich

Description

Make a large bowl of tuna at the beginning of each week and have yourself a delicious, quick lunch for every day of the week!

Ingredients:

  • mayonaise
  • relish
  • mustard
  • canned tuna
  • eggs
  • seasoning salt
  • sandwich bread
  • Ritz crackers

Steps:

  1. Place eggs in a pot and set to boil.
  2. Mix the tuna into a bowl along with mayo, mustard, and relish.
  3. Once eggs have boiled, peel them and mix them in with tuna as well.
  4. Season with seasoning salt and place in fridge to cool.
  5. Place on sandwich bread or enjoy with Ritz crackers!
